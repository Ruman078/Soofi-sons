OUR STORY

"We've been fitting Faisalabad with better sight since 1952 — and we're not done yet."

Soofi Sons & Co. (also known locally as Sufi Sons) was founded in 1952 at 33 Katchery Bazar, steps from the historic Clock Tower in the old city. Over seven decades, the business has grown from a single optical counter into a full local institution covering eye examinations, prescription eyewear, contact lenses, and hearing aid fittings.

What hasn't changed is the address, the family behind the counter, or the commitment to getting your prescription right the first time.

1952

Doors open at Katchery Bazar

Soofi Sons & Co. begins trading near the Clock Tower, Faisalabad's oldest commercial landmark.

1970s–90s

Optical dispensing expands

Frame fitting and prescription lens services grow alongside the city itself.

2000s

Contact lenses & hearing aids added

The catalogue widens to serve every stage of vision and hearing care under one roof.
